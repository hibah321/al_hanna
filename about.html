Al Hanna Healthcare Center

At Al Hanna Healthcare Center, we provide healthcare services while breaking all barriers. Our aid strives to provide the highest quality of life to patients anywhere, anytime in Dubai, through experienced, compassionate medical care.Our services include doctor-on-call, home nursing, physiotherapy, and RT-PCR Testing.

Al Hanna Center took the "Patient-Centered" approach instead of practicing the traditonal "Facility/Hospital Centered" approach. Meaning, as opposed to the patient coming to the hospital, the hospital reaches the patient.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide you with high quality healthcare while eliminating any barriers that stop you from accessing healthcare services

OUR VISION

Our vision is to provide high-quality healthcare to anyone who needs it, as well as to improve your mental and physical well-being.
